Welcome to Bottle!
Bottle is a very simple and clean designed webbrowser without overloaded functionality like Safari and the like. Actually there is, not any disturbing UI at all, you just have the clean webpages in front of you. Of course this doesnt mean no functions at all, i am trying to include all the features which you expect from your web browser. For example you can navigate by swiping on your addressbar or you just tap and hold a link to download it to your home directory. But most important is, that Bottle is intelligent. It recognizes when you need the addressbar or whether you fill in a URL or a search term. Also bottle has an included adblocker.
So stop reading now and start bottle-ing the internet ;)
